Digital Watch — Polished Requirement Specification

Overview
Let’s consider a simplified digital watch: 1. The current mode is the “Display” mode; 2. When you press once on the mode button, the watch changes to “change hour”.

Operational Context
Primary actors are not explicitly stated in the source text.

Functional Requirements
1. The system shall change mode from 'Display' to 'change hour' on a single press of the mode button.
2. The system shall increment the hour by one unit upon each press of the advance button while in 'change hour' mode.
3. The system shall change mode from 'change hour' to 'change minute' on a subsequent press of the mode button.
4. The system shall increment the minutes by one unit upon each press of the advance button while in 'change minute' mode.
5. The system shall illuminate the watch face on a press of the light button, remaining illuminated until the button is released.
6. The system shall enable an alarm feature upon activation of the alarm button.
