A person starts by either creating an account or logging in.

If the account is created, they may continue with admin-related actions. If they log in, they can continue with different roles.

If the person is an admin, they can manage user profiles by adding, editing, or removing them. They can also review articles and decide whether to publish them or reject them.

If the person is working on content, they can write an article and send it for editing. The editor reviews the article and passes it forward for approval.

If the login is not valid, the person is not allowed to continue.

Once an article is published, users can read it. While reading, they can continue reading more articles.

After reading, the user can either search for more articles or give feedback.

If feedback is given or the user finishes searching, it ends.
