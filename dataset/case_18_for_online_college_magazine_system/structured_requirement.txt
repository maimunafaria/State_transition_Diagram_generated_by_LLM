For Online College Magazine System — Polished Requirement Specification

Overview
College magazines provide an authoritative, recognizable and artistic platform of expression to the student body. However, paperback college magazines narrow the scope for a more extensive audience and are lavish of important resources like paper, especially when the magazines are printed seasonally on campus year after year. Consequently, many students may not be able to make it to the print media of the college.

Operational Context
Primary actors include student.

Functional Requirements
1. The system shall support college magazines provide an authoritative, recognizable and artistic platform of expression to the student body.
2. The system shall support consequently, many students may not be able to make it to the print media of the college.
3. The system shall enable students of the college to liberally generate content for and about the college.
4. The system shall ensure that with the help of this software, they will be able to read, write, draft, edit and publish all forms of content like articles, essays, op-eds, photography, poetry, etc.
5. The system shall support moderators will review articles before publishing to decide the pertinence of content.
6. The system shall ensure that articles will be organized based on categories appropriate to the workflow of the college.
