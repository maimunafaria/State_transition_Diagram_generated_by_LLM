Video Suggestion System — Polished Requirement Specification

Overview
When a user asks for video suggestions, the request is first received and the user is identified. After that, different types of videos are gathered at the same time. These may include videos watched by similar users, trending videos from the user’s area, videos with similar tags, videos from similar genres, and promoted videos.

Operational Context
Primary actors include user.

Functional Requirements
1. The system shall receive a video suggestion request from a user.
2. The system shall identify the user who made the video suggestion request.
3. The system shall gather different types of videos simultaneously based on the user's profile or preferences, trending videos from the user’s area, videos with similar tags, videos from similar genres, and promoted videos .
4. The system shall add additional details such as thumbnails and view counts to the suggested videos.
5. The system shall send the suggested videos to the user.
