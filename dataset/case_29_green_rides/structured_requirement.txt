Green Rides — Polished Requirement Specification

Overview
This system is useful in the road transport and highways field (Ministry of Road Transport and Highways). Green rides is a technical solution for the general public to guide them to a less polluted path while travelling, to know about the traffic jam, identification of accident- prone spots, meals- on- wheel, the establishment of drive-mode applications. This app consists of a Google map view on the main screen.

Operational Context
Primary actors include admin, user, driver, authority.

Functional Requirements
1. The system shall display a Google map view on the main screen.
2. The system shall provide multiple routes to reach a destination from a source, including traffic and pollution status updates.
3. The system shall allow users to blow a horn to all users within a specified radius.
4. The system shall send notifications to users within a specified radius when a user blows their horn.
5. The system shall allow users to report accidents to the concerned authorities.
6. The system shall categorize areas prone to accidents based on the number of accidents that have occurred there.
7. The system shall provide information about hotels and restaurants available along a chosen route.
8. The system shall send an SMS to the caller when a driver is traveling beyond a specified speed limit.
9. The system shall allow users to enable drive mode before starting their journey.
10. The system shall allow users to provide feedback on the accuracy of traffic and pollution levels in a specific area.
