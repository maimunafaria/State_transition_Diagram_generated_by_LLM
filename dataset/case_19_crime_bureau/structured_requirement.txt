Crime Bureau — Polished Requirement Specification

Overview
Rules and regulations are paramount to all aspects of life. Certain proponents have asserted that crime which is a violation against laws of the society, is integral to the human nature and hence the society can never be completely free from it. In the existing system, all work is done on papers, so it is very difficult to secure crime reports data.

Operational Context
Primary actors are not explicitly stated in the source text.

Functional Requirements
1. The system shall maintain a database of criminal records.
2. The system shall ensure the security of crime reports data.
3. The system shall automate the tracking of crime records.
4. The system shall maintain a comprehensive database of all crime activities within the state.
