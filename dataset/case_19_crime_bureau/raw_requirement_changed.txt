A user starts by entering their username and password. If the details are incorrect, they try again until they are able to log in.

After logging in, the user can choose what they want to access. They may request an FIR, request a petition, or view the list of wanted and missing persons.

Once they choose an option, the requested information is shown, and it ends.

A police user also logs in by entering their username and password. If the details are incorrect, they keep trying until they can log in.

After logging in, the police user can either view the list of wanted and missing persons or perform major activities such as adding station details and crime records.

Once the selected activity is completed, it ends.
