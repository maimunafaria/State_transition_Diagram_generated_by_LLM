A person starts by choosing whether they are a new user or an already registered user.

If they are new, they go through registration by entering their details. If the details are not valid, it ends there. If everything is correct, their information is saved, and they can move on to log in.

If they are already registered, they directly log in by entering their username and password. Once the login is successful, they continue.

After logging in, the person can manage their profile. They can edit their profile details, upload or accept requests, search for other users, or view current deals.

After finishing any of these actions, they log out, and it ends.
