Car Care — Polished Requirement Specification

Overview
Most of the car owners do not know whether their car is at risk and needs to be serviced. This considerably increases the risk of malfunctioning of major parts of a car such as the braking system and engine which lead to accidents. Car Care is a multi-brand car service and maintenance application that maintains the date at which the specific part of a car is installed and notifies the expiry date of the parts of the car for both the driver and authorized service centre, so that the car can be serviced.

Operational Context
