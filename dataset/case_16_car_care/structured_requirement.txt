Car Care — Polished Requirement Specification

Overview
Most of the car owners do not know whether their car is at risk and needs to be serviced. This considerably increases the risk of malfunctioning of major parts of a car such as the braking system and engine which lead to accidents. Car Care is a multi-brand car service and maintenance application that maintains the date at which the specific part of a car is installed and notifies the expiry date of the parts of the car for both the driver and authorized service centre, so that the car can be serviced.

Operational Context
Primary actors include driver.

Functional Requirements
1. The system shall maintain the date at which specific parts of a car are installed.
2. The system shall notify the expiry dates of car parts to both the driver and authorized service centre.
3. The system shall enable car owners and service centres to separately login and enter their cars' information.
4. The system shall allow a service centre employee to enter estimated expiry dates of parts of the car after issuing the car.
5. The system shall notify car owners and service centres when the expiry date for parts of the car is reached.
6. The system shall enable a service centre employee to contact the owner about a service request after notification.
7. The system shall allow an owner to select damaged parts to be serviced through the app.
8. The system shall enable a service centre employee to update information about changed parts in the app.
9. The system shall allow online payment for services through the app.
