A person begins at the homepage and uses the navigation bar to choose what they want to see.

They may look at projects, available departments, the history of the firm, news and blogs, or career opportunities.

If they choose to look at projects, they can either view photos or watch videos.

If they choose career opportunities, they may either view updates or open the application form for employment or internship. After filling in the form, the application is checked.

After any of these activities, updates are shown, and then it ends.
