Website Development — Polished Requirement Specification

Functional Requirements
1. The system shall allow users to navigate from the homepage using a navigation bar.
2. The system shall display information about projects when selected by the user.
3. The system shall allow users to choose between viewing photos or watching videos when viewing projects.
4. The system shall display information about career opportunities when selected by the user.
5. The system shall allow users to choose between viewing updates or opening an application form for employment or internship when viewing career opportunities.
6. The system shall check submitted job applications after they are submitted.
