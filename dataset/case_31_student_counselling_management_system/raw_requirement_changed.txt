A student starts by logging in.

If the login details are incorrect, they try again. If the login is successful, they proceed with registration and payment.

If the registration payment is not completed, it ends there. If it is completed, the student is registered and ranks are declared.

After the ranks are announced, the student pays the enrollment fees. If the payment is not successful, it ends. If the payment is successful, the student can fill in their choices.

While filling choices, if there is an issue, it ends. If everything is correct, the choices are locked and seats are allotted based on rank.

Once a seat is allotted, an admit letter is generated. The student then decides whether to accept or decline it.

After that, it ends.
