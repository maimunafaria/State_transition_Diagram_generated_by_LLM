Atm Many Scenarios — Polished Requirement Specification

Overview
The initial state of the ATM statechart is Idle. Consider the scenario consisting of the customer inserting the card into the ATM, entering the PIN, and then selecting cash withdrawal. When the Card Inserted event arrives, the ATM statechart transitions from the Idle state to the Waiting for PIN state, during which time the ATM is waiting for the customer to input the PIN.

Operational Context
Primary actors include customer.

Functional Requirements
1. The system shall allow when the Card Inserted event arrives, the ATM statechart transitions from the Idle state to the Waiting for PIN state, during which time the ATM is waiting for the customer to input the PIN.
2. The system shall support when the PIN Entered event arrives, the ATM transitions to the Validating PIN state.
3. The system shall allow assuming that the card and PIN validation is successful (event Valid PIN), the ATM transitions into Waiting for Customer Choice state.
4. The system shall ensure that it is possible to have more than one transition out of a state, with each transition caused by a different event.
5. The system shall support consider the alternative transitions that could result from PIN validation.
6. The system shall support if the two PIN numbers match, the ATM makes the Valid PIN transition to the Waiting for Customer Choice state.
7. The system shall allow if the PIN numbers do not match, the ATM makes the Invalid PIN transition to re-enter the Waiting for PIN state and prompts the customer to enter a different PIN number.
8. The system shall ensure that if the customer-entered PIN is invalid after the third attempt, the ATM makes the Third Invalid PIN transition to the Confiscating state, which results in the card being confiscated.
9. The system shall ensure that the ATM also transitions to the same state if the ATM card is reported lost or stolen during card validation, or if the card has expired.
10. The system shall ensure that the customer may decide to enter Cancel in any of the three states Waiting for PIN, Validating PIN, or Waiting for Customer Choice, which results in the statechart entering the Ejecting state, the ATM card being ejected, and the transaction terminated.
11. The system shall ensure that it is also possible for the same event to occur in a different state and have a different effect.
12. The system shall support when the Cash Dispensed event arrives, the ATM transitions to the Printing state to print the receipt.
