Device Handout — Polished Requirement Specification

Overview
Amidst this pandemic period, students are being educated through online education platforms on smartphones and other similar devices. Many underprivileged children are unable to get access to such devices. Also, the people who are willing to help aren’t able to reach the needy due to lockdown.

Operational Context
Primary actors include admin, user, students, donor, volunteers.

Functional Requirements
1. The system shall ensure that many underprivileged children are unable to get access to such devices.
2. The system shall ensure that also, the people who are willing to help aren’t able to reach the needy due to lockdown.
3. The system shall ensure that the ultimate aim of this system is to help such children by giving the collected devices from the people who were willing to help them.
4. The system shall ensure that requests the donors to enter the details regarding the device they are willing to donate.
5. The system shall support it also requests the address details of the donor in order to reach them.
6. The system shall support on successful entry of such details, the user has to wait for the approval of the device.
7. The system shall support the admin has to verify the details of the device and assign it to a pickup executive for collection.
8. The system shall support also allows the volunteers to join the team.
