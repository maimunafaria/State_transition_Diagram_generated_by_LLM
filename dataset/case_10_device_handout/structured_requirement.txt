During the pandemic period, online education is conducted through smartphones and similar devices. Many underprivileged children do not have access to such devices. At the same time, individuals who are willing to donate devices face difficulty in reaching the needy due to lockdown restrictions.

This system is designed to collect devices from donors and distribute them to underprivileged children. Donors are required to enter details about the device they wish to donate along with their address information. After submitting the details, the donor must wait for approval.

The admin verifies the device details and, upon approval, assigns a pickup executive to collect the device. If the device does not meet verification requirements, it is rejected. The system also allows volunteers to join and assist in collecting devices within their locality.

Functionalities

Donor registration

Entering device and contact details

Device verification

Approval or rejection

Assignment to pickup executive

Device collection

Volunteer participation
