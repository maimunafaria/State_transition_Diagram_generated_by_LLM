In the ATM StateMachine, the states `VerifyCard` and `OutOfService`, along with the transition from `VerifyTransaction` to `ReleaseCard`, are marked as final. This means these cannot be redefined in any extensions of the ATM StateMachine.

An extended version of the ATM StateMachine (Figure 14.39) redefines the composite state `ReadAmount` by adding a new state `EnterAmount`. This allows users to enter a desired amount, and introduces two new transitions: one leading into `EnterAmount` and another exiting from it. The states `SelectedAmount` and `FinalState` from `ReadAmount` are also redefined to serve as the source or target for these new transitions. Additionally, the state `VerifyTransaction` is redefined to act as the source of a transition that targets the newly added `EnterAmount`.

Further extensions (Figure 14.40) involve adding more transitions to the StateMachine shown in Figure 14.39 without altering the finality constraints mentioned earlier.
