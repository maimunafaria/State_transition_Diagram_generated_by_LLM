Farm Management System — Polished Requirement Specification

Overview
In India, 1 out of 4 farmers own a smartphone. But they don’t use it optimally for insights on farming based on real-time data – such as weather, soil conditions, market conditions, labour availability etcetera, which can allow them to make data-driven decisions to get the best possible results. Current technologies that solve such issues are either paid services or inaccurate.
An application if developed should primarily aim at reducing the risks, reducing the costs, improving yield, and most importantly, easy for anyone to interpret (Figures 32.1–32.8).
