Unnamed Case — Polished Requirement Specification

Overview
To provide an online platform to manage the buying, selling and storage of artistic creations provided by creators/other platforms or auctions and to organize events to exhibit these products. The system is a gallery having art pieces with its description, price and other information and the order activity, payment activity, order update activity and so on. The objective is to provide an online platform through which museums and art galleries can make use-off to exhibit their collections in times of unavailable street galleries.

Operational Context
Primary actors include admin, customer, user, authority.

Functional Requirements
1. The system shall provide an online platform for managing the buying and selling of artistic creations.
2. The system shall allow users to view art pieces along with their descriptions, prices, and other relevant information.
3. The system shall enable activities related to orders, payments, and updates of orders.
4. The system shall allow admin users to update the information of art pieces, including prices, descriptions, and stock availability.
5. The system shall enable admin users to generate reports on gallery activities.
6. The system shall allow customers to place orders for art pieces, including payment options.
7. The system shall enable customers to delete their orders.
8. The system shall allow customers to view details of art pieces.
