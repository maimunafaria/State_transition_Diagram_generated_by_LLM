Digital Watch — Polished Requirement Specification

Overview
Let’s consider a simplified digital watch: 1. The current mode is the “Display” mode; 2. When you press once on the mode button, the watch changes to “change hour”.

Operational Context
Primary actors are not explicitly stated in the source text.

Functional Requirements
1. The system shall support when you press once on the mode button, the watch changes to “change hour”.
2. The system shall ensure that every time you press the advance button, the hour is incremented by a unit; 3.
3. The system shall support when you press the mode button again, the watch changes to “change minute”.
4. The system shall ensure that every time you press on the advance button, the minutes are incremented by a unit.
5. The system shall ensure that • A light button; by pressing it, the watch face is lit until the button is released; • An alarm button, which adds a standard feature to the digital watch, as described in the first exercise of this chapter (alarm clock).
