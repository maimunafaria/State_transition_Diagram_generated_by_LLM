Cash Register — Polished Requirement Specification

Overview
This exercise concerns a simplified system of a supermarket cash register. It is inspired to a great extent by the case study of C. Larman’s first book (the Point-ofSale System), which formed the basis of the Valtech training about OOAD.

Operational Context
Primary actors include customer.

Functional Requirements
1. The system shall support when the payment transaction is finished, the cash register sends the information on the number of items sold to the stock management system.
2. The system shall support every morning, the shop manager initialises the cash registers for the day.
