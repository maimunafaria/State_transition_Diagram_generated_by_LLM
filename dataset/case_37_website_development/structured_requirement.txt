Website Development — Polished Requirement Specification

Overview
Mega Associates is an evolving architectural firm that is facing a fierce competition. The competitors have a modern, well-built website that is pulling some of the firm’s clients towards themselves. In order to avoid a situation like this and to retain all the firm’s clients and to attract new ones, a refreshing website is needed.

Operational Context
Primary actors are not explicitly stated in the source text.

Functional Requirements
1. The system shall display a list of the firm's major projects on the website.
2. The system shall provide a brief overview of all available departments and collaborations on the website.
3. The system shall display recent news related to the firm on the website.
4. The system shall provide access to the firm's insights on the website.
5. The system shall include a section detailing the history of the firm on the website.
6. The system shall provide a mail or chat feature for user interaction on the website.
7. The system shall display information about career opportunities related to the firm on the website.
