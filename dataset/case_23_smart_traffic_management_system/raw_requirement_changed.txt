A smart traffic system keeps watching the road conditions.

When there are vehicles on the road or people waiting to cross, it checks the traffic situation and responds accordingly. It may control the street lights, stop vehicles so pedestrians can cross, manage the traffic signal, or handle a medical emergency.

When a vehicle passes through the signal or an emergency situation is identified, the vehicle number is captured and the driver’s details are checked. After that, the system checks whether any traffic rules were broken.

If no rule has been broken, it ends there. If a rule has been broken, such as overspeeding, not wearing a seat belt, or not wearing a helmet, the violation is recorded and a fine is issued.

If the fine is paid, the matter is completed. If the fine is not paid, a warning is sent. If the number of violations goes beyond the allowed limit, the license is temporarily held until the fine is cleared or the probation period is over.
