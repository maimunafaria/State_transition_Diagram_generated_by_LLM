A person starts by registering.

If the registration details are not accepted, it ends there. If the registration is successful, the person logs in.

If the login details are incorrect, they try again until the login is successful.

After logging in, the person can cancel a certificate, apply for certificates, view the profile, or update the profile.

If the person applies for certificates, they fill in the form and upload the required details. They can apply for more certificates if needed. Once the request is processed and the certificate is issued, they can continue applying again or finish there.

If the person chooses to cancel a certificate, view the profile, or update the profile, it ends after that.
