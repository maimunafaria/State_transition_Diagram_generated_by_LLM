National Health Id — Polished Requirement Specification

Overview
The Prime Minister of India has announced a new scheme of National Health ID on August 15, 2020. According to this proposal, every Indian will get a unique Health ID based on their mobile number or Aadhaar number. The National Health ID will be a repository of all health-related information of a person.

Operational Context
Primary actors include patient, doctors, authority.

Functional Requirements
1. The system shall create a unique Health ID based on mobile number or Aadhaar number.
2. The system shall store all health-related information of a person in the National Health ID repository.
3. The system shall allow patients to create their Health ID digitally.
4. The system shall link each Health ID with a health data consent manager, such as the National Digital Health Mission (NDHM).
5. The system shall seek patient's consent for the seamless flow of health information from the Personal Health Records module.
