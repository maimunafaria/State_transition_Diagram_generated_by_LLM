National Health Id — Polished Requirement Specification

Overview
The Prime Minister of India has announced a new scheme of National Health ID on August 15, 2020. According to this proposal, every Indian will get a unique Health ID based on their mobile number or Aadhaar number. The National Health ID will be a repository of all health-related information of a person.

Operational Context
Primary actors include patient, doctors, authority.

Functional Requirements
1. The system shall allow according to this proposal, every Indian will get a unique Health ID based on their mobile number or Aadhaar number.
2. The system shall allow according to the National Health Authority (NHA), every patient who wishes to have their health records available digitally must start by creating a Health ID.
3. The system shall ensure that each Health ID will be linked to a health data consent manager – such as the National Digital Health Mission (NDHM) which will be used to seek the patient’s consent and allow for seamless flow of health information from the Personal Health Records module.
