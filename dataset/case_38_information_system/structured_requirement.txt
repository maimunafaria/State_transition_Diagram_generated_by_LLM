Information System — Polished Requirement Specification

Overview
iLet's suppose that an organisation wants to improve its information system and, first of all, wishes to model the training process of its employees so that some of their tasks may be computerised. 1. The training process is initialised when the training manager receives a training request on behalf of an employee.

Operational Context
Primary actors are not explicitly stated in the source text.

Functional Requirements
1. The system shall allow a training manager to receive a training request on behalf of an employee.
2. The system shall acknowledge a training request and forward agreement or disagreement to the person interested.
3. The system shall allow a training manager to look up registered courses corresponding to a training request.
4. The system shall inform an employee about the course content and suggest subsequent sessions.
5. The system shall allow a training manager to enrol an entrant in a session with the relevant training body.
6. The system shall allow an employee to cancel an enrolment or request if something crops up.
7. The system shall require an employee to submit an assessment and attendance document after completing a training course.
8. The system shall allow a training manager to check and forward invoices from training bodies to bookkeepers.
