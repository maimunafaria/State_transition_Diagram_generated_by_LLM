Atm Many Scenarios — Polished Requirement Specification

Overview
The initial state of the ATM statechart is Idle. Consider the scenario consisting of the customer inserting the card into the ATM, entering the PIN, and then selecting cash withdrawal. When the Card Inserted event arrives, the ATM statechart transitions from the Idle state to the Waiting for PIN state, during which time the ATM is waiting for the customer to input the PIN.

Operational Context
Primary actors include customer.

Functional Requirements
1. The system shall transition from the Idle state to the Waiting for PIN state upon arrival of the Card Inserted event.
2. The system shall wait for customer input of the PIN in the Waiting for PIN state.
3. The system shall transition from the Waiting for PIN state to the Validating PIN state upon arrival of the PIN Entered event.
4. The system shall determine whether the customer-entered PIN matches the stored PIN for this card in the Validating PIN state.
5. The system shall make the Valid PIN transition to the Waiting for Customer Choice state if the customer-entered PIN matches the stored PIN.
6. The system shall make the Invalid PIN transition to re-enter the Waiting for PIN state and prompt the customer to enter a different PIN number if the PIN numbers do not match.
7. The system shall make the Third Invalid PIN transition to the Confiscating state if the customer-entered PIN is invalid after three attempts.
8. The system shall transition to the Confiscating state if the ATM card is reported lost or stolen during card validation.
9. The system shall transition to the Ejecting state and eject the ATM card if the customer decides to enter Cancel in any of the three states Waiting for PIN, Validating PIN, or Waiting for Customer Choice.
10. The system shall ignore the PIN Entered event if it arrives in the Idle state.
11. The system shall allow the customer to select withdrawal from the Waiting for Customer Choice state after successful PIN validation.
12. The system shall enter the Processing Withdrawal state upon arrival of the Withdrawal Selected event if the customer selects withdrawal.
13. The system shall transition to the Dispensing state and dispense cash if the withdrawal is approved.
14. The system shall transition to the Printing state, print the receipt, and arrive upon arrival of the Cash Dispensed event.
15. The system shall enter the Ejecting state if the receipt is printed and the card has been ejected.
16. The system shall transition to the Terminating state upon arrival of the Card Ejected event.
