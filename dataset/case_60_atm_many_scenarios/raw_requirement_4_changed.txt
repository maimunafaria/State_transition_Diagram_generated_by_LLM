The ATM begins by welcoming the user, ready for interaction. After the user has entered their details and reached the options screen, they choose to withdraw money.

The machine starts processing the request and asks the bank for approval. Once the withdrawal is approved, it dispenses the cash and updates the system.

After giving the money, the ATM prints a receipt and informs the user that the transaction is complete. It then ejects the card and shows a confirmation message.

Finally, the session ends, and after a short pause, the ATM resets itself and returns to its normal waiting condition, ready for the next user.
