Atm Many Scenarios Scenario 1 — Polished Requirement Specification

Overview
A person approaches the ATM, which is quietly waiting for use. They insert their card, and the machine prompts them to enter their PIN. After the PIN is entered, the system carefully checks whether it is correct.

Operational Context
Primary actors include user.

Functional Requirements
1. The system shall prompt the user to enter their PIN after the card is inserted.
2. The system shall verify the entered PIN against the stored information.
3. The system shall ensure that if the PIN is verified successfully, the system shall provide options to the user.
4. The system shall support upon user approval, the system shall process the withdrawal request.
5. The system shall support after dispensing cash, the system shall print a receipt for the transaction.
6. The system shall support after completing the transaction, the system shall return the user's card.
7. The system shall support after a brief moment, the system shall reset itself and become ready for the next user.
