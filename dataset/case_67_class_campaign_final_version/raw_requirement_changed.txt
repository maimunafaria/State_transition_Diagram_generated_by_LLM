A campaign begins after a manager and staff are assigned. Once it is approved and the contract is signed, the campaign becomes active.

During the campaign, two kinds of activities happen. On one side, surveys may be carried out and evaluated. On the other side, adverts are prepared, approved, scheduled, and then run. The campaign can also be adjusted if needed, such as extending it or changing the budget.

At any point, the campaign can be paused, which stops all adverts, and later resumed to continue work.

If the campaign is cancelled, the system calculates the costs and prepares a final statement. Otherwise, when all work is finished, the campaign is completed and a final statement is prepared.

After completion, payment is handled. If the amount does not match what is due, adjustments like refunds may be made. Once the correct payment is settled, the campaign is marked as paid.

Finally, the campaign is archived and all assigned staff and managers are released.
