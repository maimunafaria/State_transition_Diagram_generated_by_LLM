The microwave begins by checking if everything is working properly. If any issue is found, such as a fault in the turntable or internal components, it shows an alert and stops working.

If everything is fine, it starts cooking and continues for the set time. After finishing, it gives a short sound to indicate that the process is complete and returns to a waiting condition.

Opening the door stops the microwave immediately and makes it inactive. The user can also cancel the operation at any time, which brings the microwave back to waiting.
