My Helper — Polished Requirement Specification

Overview
This case study is helpful to people who forget their mobile phones at home as a part of their busy daily routines. Keeping tick of essentials in this fast-paced life sometimes turns out to be quite demanding. People sometimes forget their phones at home.

Operational Context
Primary actors include user.

Functional Requirements
1. The system shall allow sending an SMS from any phone to locate the device.
2. The system shall change the sound profile of the phone from silent to normal via SMS without requiring internet access.
3. The system shall enable tracking the device using an SMS.
4. The system shall allow remote access to the device without requiring internet connectivity.
