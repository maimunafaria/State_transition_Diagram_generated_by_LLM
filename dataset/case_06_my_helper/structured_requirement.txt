My Helper — Polished Requirement Specification

Overview
This case study is helpful to people who forget their mobile phones at home as a part of their busy daily routines. Keeping tick of essentials in this fast-paced life sometimes turns out to be quite demanding. People sometimes forget their phones at home.
The objective of this system is to provide help to people facing these types of problems instantly with the help of advanced technology.

Operational Context
Primary actors include user.

Functional Requirements
1. The system shall support the scope of this system is increasing every day across the entire world where everything is happening at the speed of a Formula 1 Car on a race track.
2. The system shall support the alluring features of this system are Remote Access without the Internet, Track your phone through an SMS, HassleFree as no OTP and ID PASSWORD is required and an SMS can help you change the sound profile of your phone (silent to normal) without Internet.
