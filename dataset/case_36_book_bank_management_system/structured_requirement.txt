Book Bank Management System — Polished Requirement Specification

Overview
The book bank management is a software in which a member can register themselves and then he can borrow books from the book bank or the members can buy the books from the book bank. A Book Bank lends books and magazines to member, who is registered in the system. It mainly concentrates on providing the book for authorized members only.

Operational Context
Primary actors include admin, user, citizens.

Functional Requirements
1. The system shall allow members to register themselves.
2. The system shall permit authorized members to borrow books from the book bank.
3. The system shall enable members to reserve books or magazines that are not currently available.
4. The system shall allow the book bank to create, replace, and delete information about titles, members, and reservations.
5. The system shall maintain a database to track the status and issue of borrowed books.
6. The system shall categorize books by department, general, and year of study.
7. The system shall use a bar scanner chip to track the location of books and notify administrators if a book is misplaced.
8. The system shall provide sign-in, login, and logout functionality for users.
9. The system shall enable the storage and retrieval of books.
10. The system shall allow users to purchase books.
11. The system shall enable the updating of stock levels.
12. The system shall calculate due fines for overdue books.
13. The system shall support e-book functionality.
14. The system shall enable payment processing for book purchases.
15. The system shall allow the maintenance of book records.
16. The system shall generate sales reports for book transactions.
17. The system shall manage user records and sorting.
