Startup Meet — Polished Requirement Specification

Overview
The policies and guidelines for startups vary from state to state. Also, startups related to a particular domain are emphasized in particular areas. The people with the startups ideas at different levels of implementation have to approach different firms or individuals for investment who are in their nearest business circles, if not in the same state.

Operational Context
Primary actors are not explicitly stated in the source text.

Functional Requirements
1. The system shall support the policies and guidelines for startups vary from state to state.
2. The system shall ensure that also, startups related to a particular domain are emphasized in particular areas.
3. The system shall ensure that the people with the startups ideas at different levels of implementation have to approach different firms or individuals for investment who are in their nearest business circles, if not in the same state.
4. The system shall ensure that startup meet is a platform for people to privately approach firms or angel investors with their ideas implemented at different levels for funding and mentorship.
5. The system shall ensure that if people are interested in the idea a virtual presentation can be given about it to them.
6. The system shall support the entrepreneur has to request investors or mentors from different states for submitting his ideas.
