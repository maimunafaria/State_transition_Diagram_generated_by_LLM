Startup Meet — Polished Requirement Specification

Overview
The policies and guidelines for startups vary from state to state. Also, startups related to a particular domain are emphasized in particular areas. The people with the startups ideas at different levels of implementation have to approach different firms or individuals for investment who are in their nearest business circles, if not in the same state.

Operational Context
Primary actors are not explicitly stated in the source text.

Functional Requirements
1. The system shall allow entrepreneurs to privately approach firms or angel investors with their ideas implemented at different levels for funding and mentorship.
2. The system shall provide profiles for investors and entrepreneurs where they describe what they are looking for.
3. The system shall enable entrepreneurs to request investors or mentors from different states for submitting their ideas.
4. The system shall allow investors to call for a presentation of an idea if they like it.
5. The system shall enable mentors to provide mentorship.
6. The system shall allow users to view and apply for government loans and policies through the portal.
