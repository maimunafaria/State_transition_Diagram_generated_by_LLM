When a staff member joins, they become employed and can be assigned responsibilities.

At first, they may not be linked to any client or campaign. Over time, they can be assigned to a client or added to a campaign, depending on the work they are given. These assignments can also be removed, returning them to an unassigned situation.

While working, the staff member may move in and out of different assignments as needed. Eventually, when they leave the job, they are no longer employed.

After some time, their record is archived and they become a former employee.
