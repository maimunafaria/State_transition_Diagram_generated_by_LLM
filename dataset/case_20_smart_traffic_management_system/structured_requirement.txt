Smart Traffic Management System — Polished Requirement Specification

Overview
In our modern metropolitan city, people don’t follow the traffic rules. The outcome of it is unfortunate. In India, 64.4% of the total deaths are accounted by road accidents.
Smart traffic system aims in minimizing the risks of accidents by precautionary taking actions on people who violate the traffic rules.

Operational Context
Primary actors include driver.

Functional Requirements
1. The system shall support cameras to monitor the roads with high traffic levels in order to facilitate the traffic signal.
2. The system shall support helmet and seat belt detection using image processing to warn the drivers.
3. The system shall ensure that details of the driver are submitted to the traffic police, when he/she violates the law more than two times via the number plate which is captured by cameras.
4. The system shall ensure that street lights are operated based on the population density on road at night, thereby saving electricity.
5. The system shall ensure that license will be cancelled as penalty, if the law is violated.
6. The system shall ensure that details of the driver will be passed on to successive signals on account of speeding.
7. The system shall ensure that automated traffic lights regulate the traffic flow in order to avoid congestions.
