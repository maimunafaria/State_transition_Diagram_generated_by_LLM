Smart Traffic Management System — Polished Requirement Specification

Overview
In our modern metropolitan city, people don’t follow the traffic rules. The outcome of it is unfortunate. In India, 64.4% of the total deaths are accounted by road accidents.

Operational Context
Primary actors include driver.

Functional Requirements
1. The system shall monitor roads with high traffic levels to facilitate traffic signals.
2. The system shall warn drivers using helmet and seat belt detection via image processing.
3. The system shall submit details of the driver to the traffic police via the number plate when the driver violates the law more than two times.
4. The system shall operate street lights based on population density at night to save electricity.
5. The system shall cancel the license as a penalty for violating the law.
6. The system shall pass details of the driver to successive signals in case of speeding.
7. The system shall regulate traffic flow using automated traffic lights to avoid congestions.
