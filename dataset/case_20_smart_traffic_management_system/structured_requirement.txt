Smart Traffic Management System — Polished Requirement Specification

Overview
In our modern metropolitan city, people don’t follow the traffic rules. The outcome of it is unfortunate. In India, 64.4% of the total deaths are accounted by road accidents.
Smart traffic system aims in minimizing the risks of accidents by precautionary taking actions on people who violate the traffic rules.

Operational Context
Primary actors include driver.

Functional Requirements
1. The system shall support cameras to monitor the roads with high traffic levels in order to facilitate the traffic signal.
