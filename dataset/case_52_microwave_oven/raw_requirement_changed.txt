The microwave starts by showing the current time while it waits for the user. The user can choose the power level, either full power or half power, and can switch between them if needed.

After selecting the power, the user sets the cooking time by entering a number. Once the time is set and the door is closed, the microwave becomes ready to start.

If the door is open, the microwave cannot operate and stays inactive. Once the door is closed again, it becomes ready.

When the user starts the microwave, it begins cooking. If the door is opened during operation, the microwave stops immediately and becomes inactive. The user can also cancel the process, which brings the microwave back to waiting.

After stopping or cancelling, the microwave returns to showing the time and waits for the next use.
