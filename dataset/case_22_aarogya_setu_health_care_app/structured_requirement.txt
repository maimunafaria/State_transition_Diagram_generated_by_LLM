Aarogya Setu Health Care App — Polished Requirement Specification

Overview
Coronavirus pandemic is spreading in large numbers. Experts suggest that social distancing has been used for a long time as one of the methods to reduce the spike in diseases and infectious illnesses. In India alone, the cases have sharply spiked up in the past two weeks, which has led to imposing even tougher measures.

Operational Context
Primary actors include user.

Functional Requirements
1. The system shall support experts suggest that social distancing has been used for a long time as one of the methods to reduce the spike in diseases and infectious illnesses.
2. The system shall support in India alone, the cases have sharply spiked up in the past two weeks, which has led to imposing even tougher measures.
3. The system shall support by identifying ‘hotspots’, necessary mapping can help deal with the problem of community transmission, i.e., when cases start Spreading within the population in such a way that people don’t know how they were exposed to the contagion.
4. The system shall ensure that it can help people stay safe and adopt necessary precautions in some areas where there are cases and accordingly, help stop or prevent community transmission to an extent.
5. The system shall support on the basis of geotagging, it can also alert a specific user about their proximity to a nearby infection case or hotspot.
6. The system shall ensure that the app also helps users self-identify their risk and monitor their health assessment, considering the times when it can get difficult (and most of all, is not particularly safe to step out and visit health clinics).
7. The system shall ensure that while this is a noble initiative, the app also lists down basic quarantine measures for those who are considered to be in the ‘high-risk’ category.
