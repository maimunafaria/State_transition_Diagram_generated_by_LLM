A campaign starts after a manager and staff are assigned. Once everything is approved and the contract is signed, the campaign becomes active.

During the campaign, work goes through a few steps. The adverts are first prepared, then approved and scheduled, and finally they are run. If needed, the campaign can be extended or adjusted while it is running.

After all adverts have finished, the campaign is completed and a final statement is prepared.

Then payment is handled. If the amount paid is less than required, a refund is generated. If it is more, the system waits until the correct balance is settled. Once the full amount is properly paid, the campaign is marked as paid.

Finally, the campaign is archived and all assigned staff and managers are released.
