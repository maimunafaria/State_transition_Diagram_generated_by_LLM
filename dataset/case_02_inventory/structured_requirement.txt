The inventory management system is designed to address difficulties in maintaining inventory records, searching and retrieving products, managing material costs, reducing human errors, and preventing data loss. The system aims to improve data integrity, reduce customer complaints, and minimize inventory costs.

The application provides secure login access for inventory managers and salespersons. It manages goods and resources in the warehouse and supports billing operations at the counter, including discount and tax calculation.

Functionalities

Authentication login

Change password

Add goods

Remove goods

Update price

Search item

Place order to supplier

Invoice generation

Receipt generation

Discount and tax calculation

Replace item
