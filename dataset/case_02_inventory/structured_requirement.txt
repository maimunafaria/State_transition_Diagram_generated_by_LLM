Inventory — Polished Requirement Specification

Overview
The current inventory system is difficult to maintain due to the difficulty in searching and retrieving products, cost of material, human errors, less data integrity, and feasible loss of records and retrieving files. So there is a need to create robust inventory management software that ensures a reduction in customer’s complaints as well as the reduction in inventory cost. The demand for goods accrues from the growing population.

Operational Context
Primary actors include inventory managers, salespersons, customer.

Functional Requirements
1. The system shall allow inventory managers and salespersons to log in.
2. The system shall allow inventory managers and salespersons to change their passwords.
3. The system shall add goods and their corresponding details to the current inventory.
4. The system shall remove goods from the current inventory.
5. The system shall place a wholesale order with the supplier.
6. The system shall generate an invoice for the order request to the supplier.
7. The system shall allow searching of item details in the current inventory.
8. The system shall generate a bill for the customer's selected items from the inventory.
9. The system shall calculate the discount and tax based on the quantity selected by the customer.
10. The system shall replace an item in the current inventory.
11. The system shall cancel a currently generated receipt.
12. The system shall update the price details of the corresponding item.
13. The system shall log an item request from the customer.
14. The system shall generate a summary report for all transactions that have taken place.
15. The system shall notify about the removal of goods from the current inventory.
