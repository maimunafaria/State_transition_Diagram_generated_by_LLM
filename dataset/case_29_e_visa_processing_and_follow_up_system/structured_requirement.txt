Overview
A visa is a document issued by a country giving a certain individual permission to enter a country for a given period of time for certain purposes. It is received after several verifications through offline mode. It requires the applicant to go to the Passport Verification Centre for the verification of documents.

Operational Context
Primary actors include administrator, authority.
