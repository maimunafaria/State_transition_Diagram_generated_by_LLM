E Visa Processing And Follow Up System — Polished Requirement Specification

Overview
A visa is a document issued by a country giving a certain individual permission to enter a country for a given period of time for certain purposes. It is received after several verifications through offline mode. It requires the applicant to go to the Passport Verification Centre for the verification of documents.

Operational Context
Primary actors include administrator, authority.

Functional Requirements
1. The system shall allow applicants to apply for a visa from anywhere and at any time.
2. The system shall allow applicants to register with a valid email and password.
3. The system shall verify the correctness of applicant credentials prior to permitting login.
4. The system shall support after logging in, the system shall display the application form with necessary details and allow attachment of required documents.
5. The system shall present a list of available visa types for applicant selection.
6. The system shall enable applicants to view fee and requirement information for each country's visa application.
7. The system shall permit applicants to modify or delete their applied details.
8. The system shall allow administrators to view details of applicants' required visas.
9. The system shall enable administrators to update or delete applicants' details.
10. The system shall provide administrators with a means of interacting with the Visa Consultant Officer.
11. The system shall allow Visa Consultant Officers to view and verify document proofs submitted by applicants.
12. The system shall grant Visa Consultant Officers the authority to accept or reject visa applications.
