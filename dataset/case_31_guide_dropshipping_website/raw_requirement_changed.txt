A person starts by choosing whether they are a seller or a customer and then logs in.

If they are a seller, they can add or remove products from their list and finalize what they want to offer.

If they are a customer, they can view available products and choose one they are interested in.

After that, the customer and seller communicate to discuss the deal.

Based on the discussion, either the purchase is completed or it does not go ahead.

If the purchase is completed, the customer makes the payment and can later give feedback on the product.

If the deal does not happen, it ends there.
