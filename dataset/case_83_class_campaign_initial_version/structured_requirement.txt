Class Campaign Initial Version — Polished Requirement Specification

Overview
A campaign starts after a manager and staff are assigned. Once it is approved and the contract is signed, it becomes active. While running, the campaign involves two main activities.

Operational Context
Primary actors are not explicitly stated in the source text.

Functional Requirements
1. The system shall start a campaign after a manager and staff are assigned.
2. The system shall activate a campaign once it is approved and the contract is signed.
3. The system shall allow surveys to be carried out and evaluated during the campaign.
4. The system shall prepare, approve, schedule, and run adverts during the campaign.
5. The system shall allow adjusting the campaign if needed, such as extending it or changing the budget.
6. The system shall pause a campaign at any time, stopping all adverts, and later resume it to continue work.
7. The system shall calculate the costs and prepare a final statement if the campaign is cancelled.
8. The system shall complete the campaign and prepare a final statement once all activities are finished.
9. The system shall handle payment after the campaign is completed.
10. The system shall make adjustments if the payment does not match the required amount.
11. The system shall mark the campaign as paid once everything is settled.
12. The system shall archive the campaign and release all assigned staff and managers after it is completed.
