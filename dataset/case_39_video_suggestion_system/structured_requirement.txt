Video Suggestion System — Polished Requirement Specification

Overview
The Video Suggestion System is used in social networking platforms such as YouTube and Facebook. The ultimate aim of this system is to suggest the videos to the users based on the factors such as trending, similar views of the people and their search results. The Video Suggestion System will be found helpful in place of searching a video based on the preference and taste of the user and it also found that it can play a role in the entertainment sector.

Operational Context
Primary actors include user.
