Petrol Gas Pump — Polished Requirement Specification

Overview
I used an example of a model of a simple microwave oven. The rounded rectangles represent system states and the arrows represent stimuli that force a transition from one state to another. The names chosen in the state machine diagram are descriptive.

Operational Context
Primary actors include user.

Functional Requirements
1. The system shall idle in a waiting state when the pump is not operating.
2. The system shall allow the buyer to insert a credit card into a card reader built into the pump.
3. The system shall read card details when a credit card is inserted.
4. The system shall allow the buyer to remove the credit card from the card reader.
5. The system shall validate the inserted credit card.
6. The system shall initialize the pump when the credit card is validated.
7. The system shall transition to the Delivering state when the fuel hose is removed from its holster.
8. The system shall start fuel delivery when the trigger on the nozzle is activated.
9. The system shall stop fuel delivery when the trigger on the nozzle is released.
10. The system shall transition to the Paying state after fuel delivery is complete.
11. The system shall debit the user's account when payment is made.
