Petrol Gas Pump — Polished Requirement Specification

Overview
I used an example of a model of a simple microwave oven. The rounded rectangles represent system states and the arrows represent stimuli that force a transition from one state to another. The names chosen in the state machine diagram are descriptive.
The fuel delivery system is designed to allow unattended operation.

Operational Context
Primary actors include user.

Functional Requirements
1. The system shall support the rounded rectangles represent system states and the arrows represent stimuli that force a transition from one state to another.
2. The system shall ensure that the fuel delivery system is designed to allow unattended operation.
3. The system shall ensure that this causes a transition to a Reading state where the card details are read and the buyer is then asked to remove the card.
4. The system shall ensure that removal of the card triggers a transition to a Validating state where the card is validated.
5. The system shall ensure that if the card is valid, the system initializes the pump and, when the fuel hose is removed from its holster, transitions to the Delivering state, where it is ready to deliver fuel.
6. The system shall allow activating the trigger on the nozzle causes fuel to be pumped; this stops when the trigger is released (for simplicity, I have ignored the pressure switch that is designed to stop fuel spillage).
7. The system shall ensure that after the fuel delivery is complete and the buyer has replaced the hose in its holster, the system moves to a Paying state where the user’s account is debited.
8. The system shall support after payment, the pump software returns to the Waiting state.
