Diet Care — Polished Requirement Specification

Overview
Health is wealth. Health plays a major role in everyone’s life. People consume unhealthy food and ruin their diet.

Operational Context
Primary actors include user.

Functional Requirements
1. The system shall support this application benefits people with rich nutrients to improve their health.
2. The system shall allow users can provide their daily diet cycle and get good recommendations to improve it.
3. The system shall support they can also provide their medical reports to get nutrients according to their medical state.
4. The system shall support the user also gets the privilege to enter the disease name and get the nutrient benefit for fighting that disease.
