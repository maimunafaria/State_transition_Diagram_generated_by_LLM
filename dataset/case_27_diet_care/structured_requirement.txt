Diet Care — Polished Requirement Specification

Overview
Health is wealth. Health plays a major role in everyone’s life. People consume unhealthy food and ruin their diet.

Operational Context
Primary actors include user.

Functional Requirements
1. The system shall provide recommendations of nutrients to users.
2. The system shall allow users to input their daily diet cycle and provide good recommendations for improvement.
3. The system shall enable users to input their medical reports and provide nutrients based on their medical state.
4. The system shall allow users to enter a disease name and provide nutrient benefits for fighting that disease.
5. The system shall enable users to contact professional nutritionists for further clarification.
