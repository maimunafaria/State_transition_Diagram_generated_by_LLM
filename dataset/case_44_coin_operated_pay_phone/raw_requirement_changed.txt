A phone starts when it is not being used.

When someone picks up the receiver, they need to insert coins. Once enough credit is added, they can dial a number. If the number is valid, the call starts when the other person answers.

During the call, it continues as long as there is enough credit. If the credit runs out, the call ends.

At any time, the user can add more coins to continue. If they hang up, any unused coins are returned, and the phone goes back to being unused.
