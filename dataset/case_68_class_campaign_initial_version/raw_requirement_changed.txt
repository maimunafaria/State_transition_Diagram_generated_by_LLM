A campaign starts after a manager and staff are assigned. Once it is approved and the contract is signed, it becomes active.

While running, the campaign involves two main activities. Surveys can be carried out and evaluated, and adverts are prepared, approved, scheduled, and then run. The campaign can also be adjusted if needed, such as extending it or changing the budget.

The campaign may be paused at any time, stopping all adverts, and later resumed to continue work.

If the campaign is cancelled, the system calculates the costs and prepares a final statement. Otherwise, once all activities are finished, the campaign is completed and a final statement is prepared.

After that, payment is handled. If the payment does not match the required amount, adjustments are made. Once everything is settled, the campaign is marked as paid.

Finally, the campaign is archived and all assigned staff and managers are released.
