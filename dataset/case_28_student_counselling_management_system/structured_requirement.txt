Student Counselling Management System — Polished Requirement Specification

Overview
The first phase of a person’s life is confined mainly by education and training. Hence, counselling is an important aspect for the students of the current technological generation as an individual counsellor may remain in contact with all individual students as well as parents. Student Counselling Management System will simplify the process and reduce the manual paperwork.

Operational Context
Primary actors include student.

Functional Requirements
1. The system shall allow individual counsellors to maintain contact with all individual students and their parents.
2. The system shall simplify the process and minimize manual paperwork.
3. The system shall automate the manual process, converting it into a fully computerized system.
4. The system shall design and implement a system for rationalizing admissions to specific courses within universities or institutions.
5. The system shall enable students to register for counselling services if they meet the eligibility criteria.
6. The system shall provide students with information about colleges and universities where they can apply, based on their qualifications.
7. The system shall generate reports based on the student's merit list.
8. The system shall maintain a record of counselling procedures and activities.
9. The system shall publish the results of counselling services and other relevant information.
10. The system shall provide students with information about available seats within the institution, including booked and vacant seats.
