Student Counselling Management System — Polished Requirement Specification

Overview
The first phase of a person’s life is confined mainly by education and training. Hence, counselling is an important aspect for the students of the current technological generation as an individual counsellor may remain in contact with all individual students as well as parents. Student Counselling Management System will simplify the process and reduce the manual paperwork.
The main objective of the Student Counselling Management System is to design a system to rationalize the admission to particular courses in a university/institution.

Operational Context
Primary actors include student.

Functional Requirements
1. The system shall ensure that it is used to smoothen the work of each counsellor who is facing problems currently, and making complete atomization of manual process to computerized system.
2. The system shall ensure that the main objective of the Student Counselling Management System is to design a system to rationalize the admission to particular courses in a university/institution.
3. The system shall ensure that through this system, the student will be able to register for counselling if he is eligible.
4. The system shall ensure that the main features are online admission, report generation based on student’s merit list and keeping track of counselling procedures and finally publishing results.
