A person first checks whether their medical history is already available. If it is not, they fill in their personal medical history, and the information is checked and saved. After that, they can use the app.

Once inside, they can do different things. They can view their medical history, get reminders to take medicines on time, get reminders to buy medicines when they are not in stock, schedule appointments, use the emergency button, write medical notes, or view medical notes.

If they schedule an appointment, they can also send a message to the receptionist. If they use the emergency button, emergency contacts are called.

If they view their medical history, they may choose to update it. If they view their medical notes, they may also choose to update them.

They can also simply read reminders, write notes, send the message, or call emergency contacts ( after clicking on emergency button ), and finish there.
