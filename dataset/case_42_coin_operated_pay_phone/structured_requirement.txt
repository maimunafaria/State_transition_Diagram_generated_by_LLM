Coin Operated Pay Phone — Polished Requirement Specification

Overview
This case study involves a simplified system of a coin-operated pay phone. 1. The minimum cost of a call is 20 pence.

Operational Context
Primary actors include user.

Functional Requirements
1. The system shall support the pay phone uses up money as soon as the callee picks up the receiver and with each unit of time (UT) generated by the switchboard.
