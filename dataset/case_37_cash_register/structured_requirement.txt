Cash Register — Polished Requirement Specification

Overview
This exercise concerns a simplified system of a supermarket cash register. It is inspired to a great extent by the case study of C. Larman’s first book (the Point-ofSale System), which formed the basis of the Valtech training about OOAD.

Operational Context
Primary actors include customer.

Functional Requirements
1. The system shall display the price and description of each item.
2. The system shall indicate the end of the sale when all purchases are recorded.
3. The system shall display the total cost of the purchases.
4. The system shall calculate and indicate the change to be given to the customer.
5. The system shall verify the customer's financial solvency by sending a request to an authorisation centre for cheque payments.
6. The system shall send a request for authorisation to an authorisation centre for credit card payments, according to the card type.
7. The system shall record the sale and print a receipt.
8. The system shall send the information on the number of items sold to the stock management system after payment transaction is finished.
9. The system shall be initialised by the shop manager every morning for the day's operation.
