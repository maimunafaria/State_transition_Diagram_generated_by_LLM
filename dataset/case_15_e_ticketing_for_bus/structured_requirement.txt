E Ticketing For Bus — Polished Requirement Specification

Functional Requirements
1. The system shall allow a user to create a new account.
2. The system shall validate the registration of a new user.
3. The system shall allow the user to log in upon successful registration.
4. The system shall provide options for logged-in users to check their ticket status.
5. The system shall allow a user to book a ticket, which will be confirmed then make the payment.
6. The system shall enable users to manage their account details such as editing or deleting them.
7. The system shall log out the user after they complete any action.
