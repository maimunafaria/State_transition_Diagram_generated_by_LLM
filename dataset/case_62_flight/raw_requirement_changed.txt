When a flight is first created, it is scheduled and given a flight number. At this point, everything is prepared, but the flight hasn’t started yet.

Once the flight takes off, it is simply considered to be on its journey. It stays in that phase until it reaches its destination.

After landing, the flight is no longer active and becomes part of past records. Even if a flight is cancelled before it starts, it is still recorded in the same way.

Over time, when the flight is no longer important, its information is removed from the system.
