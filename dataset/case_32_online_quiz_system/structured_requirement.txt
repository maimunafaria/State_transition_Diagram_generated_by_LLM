Online Quiz System — Polished Requirement Specification

Overview
Online learning is a part of many institution’s course offerings around the world. The scope of online quiz systems increased in this pandemic situation. The objective of the system is to maintain and improve the education rate by conducting quizzes (exams).

Operational Context
Primary actors include admin, student.

Functional Requirements
1. The system shall allow teachers to define the number of questions for a quiz.
2. The system shall enable teachers to set the time of the quiz.
3. The system shall allow teachers to schedule quizzes.
4. The system shall enable teachers to edit quiz settings after the initial setup.
5. The system shall provide auto-evaluation of student performance during quizzes.
6. The system shall allow students to view the correct answers after completing a quiz.
7. The system shall enable administrators to register both teachers and students.
