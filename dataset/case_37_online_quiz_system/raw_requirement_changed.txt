A user starts by registering or logging in with their details.

After that, the user may continue as a teacher or as a student.

If the user continues as a teacher, they can create a new quiz or edit an existing one. After working on the quiz, they can save it as a draft or post it for students. Once the quiz is over, the teacher can view the result.

If the user continues as a student, they first select a quiz after getting notified. Then they answer the quiz. Once they are done or the time is over, they submit it. After submission, they can see the result and may also give feedback.

After finishing these activities, it ends.
