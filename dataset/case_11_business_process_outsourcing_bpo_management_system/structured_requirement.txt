Business Process Outsourcing Bpo Management System — Polished Requirement Specification

Overview
Login – provides a personalized profile for each user, to ensure security. Profile changes – each user can personalize their account, and change their password. Post project request – clients post their request with requirements for BPO organizations to choose from.

Operational Context
Primary actors include user.

Functional Requirements
1. The system shall provide a personalized profile for each user.
2. The system shall allow users to personalize their account and change their password.
3. The system shall enable clients to post project requests with requirements for BPO organizations.
4. The system shall allow BPO organizations to search and respond to client project requests.
5. The system shall enable the transfer of documents required by BPO organizations through the system.
6. The system shall provide progress updates to clients on work being done.
7. The system shall allow clients to check their product and quote modifications.
8. The system shall enable BPO organizations to upload their products/services for further procedure.
9. The system shall facilitate payment from clients to BPO organizations at the end of agreements.
10. The system shall enable the shipment of products/services to clients.
11. The system shall allow clients to rate the service provided by BPO organizations.
