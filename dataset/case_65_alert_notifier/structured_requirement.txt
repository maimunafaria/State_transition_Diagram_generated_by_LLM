Alert Notifier — Polished Requirement Specification

Overview
After loading, the component is initialized and, if successful, waits for updates. When an update is received the Notifier processes the update. When the Notifier receives a shutdown it is unloaded and terminates.

Operational Context
Primary actors are not explicitly stated in the source text.

Functional Requirements
1. The system shall initialize the Stock Alert Notifier component after loading.
2. The system shall wait for updates from the Stock Alert Notifier component if its initialization is successful.
3. The system shall process received updates in the Stock Alert Notifier component.
4. The system shall unload and terminate the Stock Alert Notifier component upon receipt of a shutdown signal.
