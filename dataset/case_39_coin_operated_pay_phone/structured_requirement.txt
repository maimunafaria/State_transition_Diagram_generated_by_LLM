Coin Operated Pay Phone — Polished Requirement Specification

Overview
This case study involves a simplified system of a coin-operated pay phone. 1. The minimum cost of a call is 20 pence.

Operational Context
Primary actors include user.

Functional Requirements
1. The system shall enforce a minimum cost of 20 pence for a call.
2. The system shall enforce a 2-minute time limit after inserting coins to dial a number.
3. The system shall check if the line is free or engaged before connecting the call.
4. The system shall allow the caller to hang up first.
5. The system shall deduct money as soon as the callee picks up the receiver and with each unit of time (UT) generated by the switchboard.
6. The system shall allow the caller to add more coins at any time.
7. The system shall return any unused change after hanging up.
