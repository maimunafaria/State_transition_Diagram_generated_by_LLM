Information System — Polished Requirement Specification

Overview
iLet's suppose that an organisation wants to improve its information system and, first of all, wishes to model the training process of its employees so that some of their tasks may be computerised. 1. The training process is initialised when the training manager receives a training request on behalf of an employee.

Operational Context
Primary actors are not explicitly stated in the source text.

Functional Requirements
1. The system shall support the training process is initialised when the training manager receives a training request on behalf of an employee.
2. The system shall support when the employee has reached a decision, the training manager enrols the entrant in the session with the relevant training body.
3. The system shall support if something crops up, the employee must inform the training manager as soon as possible in order to cancel the enrolment or application.
4. The system shall support at the end of the employee’s training, he or she must submit an assessment to the training manager on the training course that he or she completed, as well as a document proving his or her attendance.
5. The system shall support the training manager then checks the invoice that the training body has sent him or her before forwarding it to the bookkeeper of purchases.
6. The system shall support this request is acknowledged by the training manager who qualifies it and then forwards his agreement or disagreement to the person who is interested.
7. The system shall support in the case of agreement, the training manager looks in the catalogue of registered courses for a training course corresponding to the request.
8. The system shall support when the employee sends back his or her choice, the training manager enrols the entrant in the session with the relevant training Body 3.
9. The system shall support if something crops up, the employee must inform the training manager as soon as possible to cancel the enrolment or request.
