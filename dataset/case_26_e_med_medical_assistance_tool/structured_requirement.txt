E Med Medical Assistance Tool — Polished Requirement Specification

Overview
e-Med is your perfect medical friend. We often see our grandparents forgetting to take their medicines or getting tensed when they run out of medical supplies. In this fast-paced world with unhealthy lifestyles, a number of young people also have some medical issues.

Operational Context
Primary actors include doctor.

Functional Requirements
1. The system shall provide reminders for taking medicines.
2. The system shall send notifications to restock medicines.
3. The system shall schedule monthly or yearly appointments with the family doctor.
4. The system shall automatically contact emergency contacts in critical situations.
5. The system shall keep a record of medical history using the Medical Notes feature.
