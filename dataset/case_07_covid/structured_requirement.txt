The all-in-one COVID-19 management system digitizes the process of reducing virus load in the community. It supports tracking and treating COVID-19 patients and monitoring the spread of the virus. The system aims to help healthcare organizations and governments assess patient risk profiles and provide awareness to common people by connecting them with virtual care capabilities. Because reducing contact between people is important in the current epidemiological situation, the system digitizes major activities to reduce infection rate, support common people, and enable medical professionals to help more patients through telemedicine and virtual care.

Functionalities:

Registered Patient: Patient health information (such as temperature, symptoms, medicine intake, and diet) can be updated. Patient information from screening to discharge can be viewed, and medicines can be prescribed to a specific patient.

COVID-19 Patients: Patients can view data shared by the doctor. Home-quarantined patients can update data and use a checklist of medical shops in their locality.

Common People: People can check the number of infected persons (symptomatic and asymptomatic) and deaths in their locality (current street, 500 m, 1 km, 5 km, 10 km). They can also view blogs where recovered persons share their experience.

COVID-19 Essentials Seller: Sellers can stock essentials such as masks and gloves according to infection in an area, and nearby medical shops will be listed.

Corporation Worker Head: Workers can be assigned to disinfect COVID-affected areas regularly based on infection data and update status.

Admin: The admin updates the current number of infected people in a locality.
