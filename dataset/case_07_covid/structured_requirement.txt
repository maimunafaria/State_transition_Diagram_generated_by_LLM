Covid — Polished Requirement Specification

Overview
The all in one COVID-19 management system simplifies the entire process of decreasing the virus load in the community by digitalization. It includes tracking and treating COVID-19 patients and monitoring the spread of the virus. It aims to assist healthcare organizations and governments to assess patient risk profiles and provide awareness to common people by connecting them with virtual care capabilities.

Operational Context
Primary actors include admin, patient, doctor, seller.

Functional Requirements
1. The system shall track temperature, symptoms, medicine intake, and diet of registered patients.
2. The system shall view all information of patients from screening to discharge.
3. The system shall prescribe medicines to specific patients.
4. The system shall allow COVID-19 patients to update data and checklist of medical shops in their locality.
5. The system shall display the number of people infected (symptomatic and asymptomatic) and death in the user's locality.
6. The system shall provide a blog where recovered persons can share their experience.
7. The system shall allow COVID-19 Essentials Sellers to stock up essentials like masks and gloves according to infection in an area.
8. The system shall list medical shops nearby the area for COVID-19 Essentials Sellers.
9. The system shall assign workers to disinfect COVID-affected areas regularly based on infection data for Corporation Worker Heads.
10. The system shall allow the Admin to update the current number of infected people in a locality.
