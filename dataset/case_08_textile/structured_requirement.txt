The Textile Management System is developed to monitor and manage all processes in a textile manufacturing industry. The system covers order receiving, purchasing of raw materials, classification and processing of raw materials, production of garments, and selling of finished products.

The objective of the system is to ensure reliable data handling and simplify the manufacturing and transportation processes of garments and raw materials. It reduces the difficulties present in manual systems by minimizing errors in data management.

Functionalities

Customer registration

Receiving garment orders

Sampling

Stock checking

Purchasing raw materials

Processing raw materials

Production of garments

Product checking and clearance

Shipment

Billing

Report generation
