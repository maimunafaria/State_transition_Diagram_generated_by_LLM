Textile — Polished Requirement Specification

Overview
The ‘Textile Management System’ case study is designed to monitor all the processes in a textile manufacturing industry. There are various processes such as order receiving, purchase of raw materials, classification of raw materials, production of goods and selling the products. The main aim of this system is to ensure the reliability of data and to ease the process of manufacturing and transportation of garments and raw materials.

Operational Context
Primary actors include customer.

Functional Requirements
1. The system shall monitor all processes in a textile manufacturing industry.
2. The system shall ensure the reliability of data.
3. The system shall ease the process of manufacturing and transportation of garments and raw materials.
4. The system shall reduce errors in data handling.
5. The system shall support customer registration.
6. The system shall support sampling.
7. The system shall support stock checking.
8. The system shall support purchasing and processing of raw materials.
9. The system shall support production of garments.
10. The system shall support product checking and clearance.
11. The system shall support shipment.
12. The system shall support billing.
13. The system shall generate reports.
