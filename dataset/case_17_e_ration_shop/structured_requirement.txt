E Ration Shop — Polished Requirement Specification

Overview
Public Distribution System (PDS) is an Indian food security system. It is established by the Government of India under the Ministry of Consumer Affairs, Food, and Public Distribution and managed jointly with state governments in India. The traditional PDS is used to distribute grocery items to India’s poor who are valid ration cardholders.
Here, we are going to make a website for shopping purposes.

Operational Context
Primary actors include admin, users.

Functional Requirements
1. The system shall ensure that the traditional PDS is used to distribute grocery items to India’s poor who are valid ration cardholders.
2. The system shall ensure that in order to make it efficient and improve the current system of PDS, we are implementing e-Ration Shop.
3. The system shall ensure that here, we are going to make a website for shopping purposes.
4. The system shall ensure that the main reason for using this website is to make this process computerized and to remove the drawbacks of the present way of issuing products based on the ration card.
5. The system shall ensure that the main drawback in the current system is that the PDS has been criticized for its urban bias and its failure to serve the poorer sections of the population effectively.
6. The system shall support also, many retail shopkeepers have a large number of bogus cards to sell food grains in the open market.
7. The system shall ensure that many PDS dealers resort to malpractice since they acquire less Salary so, by this project we are solving this problem too.
8. The system shall ensure that what’s meant for them or the farm produce procured by the PDS is diverted to the open market.
9. The system shall ensure that so in order to avoid all these drawbacks, we are going to use the e-Ration Shop which will help us to avoid the corruption in PDS if not eradicate it.

The system shall ensure that the various importance of the proposed case study is every shop’s geo-location will be located on the map, all the information regarding shop and shopkeeper respective to the ration card and region, shop opening and closing details will be available, stock provided based on category, retailers can add, edit, delete and update their shop’s other items also, retailers can advertise their shops, stock availability will be notified on regular basis, product delivery feature, complaint forum for complaints by users, easy payment system by PayTM, COD etc., admin and moderator features for government and shopkeepers and authentication system by Aadhaar number and One-Time Pin (OTP) .
