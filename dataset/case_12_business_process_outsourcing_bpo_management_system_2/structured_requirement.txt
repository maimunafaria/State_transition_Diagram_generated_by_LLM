Business Process Outsourcing Bpo Management System Scenario 2 — Polished Requirement Specification

Overview
When a person wants to search for users, they can start by choosing how they want to filter the results. They can either select a category or choose a price range. Once the selection is made, the system shows the matching users based on that choice, and it ends.

Operational Context
Primary actors include users.

Functional Requirements
1. The system shall allow users to search by selecting a filter category.
2. The system shall allow users to search by selecting a price range as a filter.
3. The system shall display matching users based on the chosen criteria after the user selects a filter.
