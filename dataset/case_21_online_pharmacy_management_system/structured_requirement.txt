Online Pharmacy Management System — Polished Requirement Specification

Overview
A customer starts by searching for a product. After finding something they like, they can add it to their wishlist and then move it to the cart. Once the item is in the cart, they provide or update their delivery details.

Operational Context
Primary actors include customer.

Functional Requirements
1. The system shall allow customers to search for products.
2. The system shall allow customers to add products they like to their wishlist.
3. The system shall allow customers to move items from their wishlist to the shopping cart.
4. The system shall allow customers to provide or update delivery details for items in the cart.
5. The system shall allow customers to place orders after providing delivery details.
6. The system shall enable customers to track their orders after placement.
7. The system shall allow customers to complete payment as part of the ordering process.
