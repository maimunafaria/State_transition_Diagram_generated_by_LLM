Job Seeker Portal System — Polished Requirement Specification

Overview
Job seekers work hard to gain the right skills and knowledge to give them an edge over others in the role they seek. However, at times, despite their best efforts, one may find it difficult to move a step closer to this sought-after job. Hence a platform for listing out the availability of jobs irrespective of the field is required.
Furthermore, a job site serves a dual purpose.

Operational Context
Primary actors are not explicitly stated in the source text.

Functional Requirements
1. The system shall support job seekers work hard to gain the right skills and knowledge to give them an edge over others in the role they seek.
2. The system shall support however, at times, despite their best efforts, one may find it difficult to move a step closer to this sought-after job.
3. The system shall support on one hand, it lists out the availability of jobs to candidates, and on the other, it serves as a database of registered candidate’s profiles for companies to shortlist.
4. The system shall ensure that the objective is to develop a software solution to predict the availability of jobs based on location, sectors, package, platform, interest and eligibility.
5. The system shall ensure that as it is important to keep the candidates engaged during their job search, it is important to provide facets on the abovementioned criteria so that they can narrow down to the jobs of their choice .
