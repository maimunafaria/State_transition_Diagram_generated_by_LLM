Job Seeker Portal System — Polished Requirement Specification

Overview
Job seekers work hard to gain the right skills and knowledge to give them an edge over others in the role they seek. However, at times, despite their best efforts, one may find it difficult to move a step closer to this sought-after job. Hence a platform for listing out the availability of jobs irrespective of the field is required.

Operational Context
Primary actors include companies, job seekers.

Functional Requirements
1. The system shall display job listings from different fields.
2. The system shall allow searching job listings by location, sectors, package, platform, interest and eligibility criteria.
3. The system shall enable registration of candidate profiles for company use in shortlisting.
4. The system shall provide filtering options based on location, sectors, package, platform, interest and eligibility criteria to assist candidates in narrowing down job search results.
