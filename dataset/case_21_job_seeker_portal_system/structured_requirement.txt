Job Seeker Portal System — Polished Requirement Specification

Overview
Job seekers work hard to gain the right skills and knowledge to give them an edge over others in the role they seek. However, at times, despite their best efforts, one may find it difficult to move a step closer to this sought-after job. Hence a platform for listing out the availability of jobs irrespective of the field is required.
Furthermore, a job site serves a dual purpose.
