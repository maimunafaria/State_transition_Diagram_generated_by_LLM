A person first enters their details so the kind of user can be identified.

If the person is a citizen, they begin by registering. If the registration is valid, they log in. If the login details are wrong, they keep trying until they are correct.

After logging in, the citizen can upload documents, view their profile, or apply for a certificate.

If they upload documents, they can keep uploading more if needed, or stop there.

If they apply for a certificate, they can apply for more than one. They can also send the request to the issuer, after which the certificate is provided to them, and then they may continue applying again.

If the person is a registrar, they can ask for a certificate for verification. They can keep asking for more certificates, send a request to the digital locker, receive the certificate, and continue again if needed.

At any point, once the person is done, it ends.
