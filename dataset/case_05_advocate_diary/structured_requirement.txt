This project is designed to support lawyers in managing their work more efficiently. In countries with large populations, the need for law and enforcement is significant, and lawyers often maintain their case records as hard copies. This web application provides a digital platform to help lawyers manage and organize their professional data in soft copy format.

The system is a responsive web application that offers various services to assist lawyers in maintaining their documents and case-related information.

System Functionalities
1. Case Logbook and Calendar

The application provides a logbook that contains details of cases handled by the lawyer. It also includes a calendar to manage case schedules and related events.

2. Document Upload and Storage

Lawyers can upload and store documents related to their cases within the system.

3. Model Forms and Online Filing

The system provides formats of model forms and includes links to online filing services.

4. Legal References

The application offers quick references for common cases and detailed legal information through e-books.

5. Judicial Examination Information

Information related to judicial examinations for lawyers is made available within the application.

6. News, Blogs, and Feedback

The system includes news updates, blogs, and a feedback feature for users.
