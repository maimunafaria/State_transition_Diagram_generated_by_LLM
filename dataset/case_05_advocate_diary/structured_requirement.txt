Advocate Diary — Polished Requirement Specification

Overview
The need for law and enforcement in a country with a high population like India is salient. Lawyers play a major role in maintaining most of their data as hard copies. This project is mainly designed for lawyers to make their work more easy and efficient.

Operational Context
Primary actors are not explicitly stated in the source text.

Functional Requirements
1. The system shall provide a logbook with details about cases.
2. The system shall allow lawyers to upload and save documents related to their case.
3. The system shall contain formats of model forms.
4. The system shall provide links to online filing.
5. The system shall provide quick references of common cases.
6. The system shall contain detailed information about laws through e-books.
7. The system shall provide information about judicial exams for lawyers.
8. The system shall contain news updates for lawyers, blogs, and feedback.
