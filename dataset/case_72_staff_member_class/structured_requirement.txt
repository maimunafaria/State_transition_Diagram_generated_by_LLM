Staff Member Class — Polished Requirement Specification

Overview
When a staff member joins, they become employed and can be assigned responsibilities. At first, they may not be linked to any client or campaign. Over time, they can be assigned to a client or added to a campaign, depending on the work they are given.

Operational Context
Primary actors are not explicitly stated in the source text.

Functional Requirements
1. The system shall allow assigning a staff member to a client or adding them to a campaign.
2. The system shall allow removing assignments from staff members, returning them to an unassigned state.
3. The system shall enable staff members to switch between different assignments during their work period.
4. The system shall mark a staff member as unemployed when they leave the job.
5. The system shall archive the record of a former employee after some time has passed.
