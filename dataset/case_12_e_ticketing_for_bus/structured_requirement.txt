E Ticketing For Bus — Polished Requirement Specification

Overview
In this case study, a software package to automate the manual procedures of reserving a bus ticket is made through Software Maintenance and Development Center (SMDC) Travels. Specifically, the objectives of this case study consist of providing a web-based bus ticket reservation function where a customer can buy a bus ticket through the online system without a need to queue up at the counter to purchase a bus ticket, enabling customers to check the availability and types of buses online.

Operational Context
Primary actors include admin, customer, user.

Functional Requirements
1. The system shall provide a web-based bus ticket reservation function.
2. The system shall allow customers to check the availability and types of buses online.
3. The system shall enable customers to check the time departure for every Indian Tourist Centre (ITC) bus.
4. The system shall ease bus ticket payment by obtaining a bank pin after payments are made to the various designated banks.
5. The system shall allow customers to cancel their reservation.
6. The system shall provide admin user privileges for updating and cancelling payment, route, and vehicle records.
