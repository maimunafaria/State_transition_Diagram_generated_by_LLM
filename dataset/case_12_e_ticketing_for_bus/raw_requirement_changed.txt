A person starts by either creating a new account or logging in if they already have one.

If they are new, they go through registration. If the registration is not valid, it ends there. If it is successful, they continue to log in.

After logging in, the person can choose what they want to do.

They can check the status of their tickets and then finish.

They can book a ticket, after which the ticket is confirmed, and then they make the payment.

They can also choose to manage their account, such as editing or deleting their details.

After completing any of these actions, the person logs out, and it ends.
