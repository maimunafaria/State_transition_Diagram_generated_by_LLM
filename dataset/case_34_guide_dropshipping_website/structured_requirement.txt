Guide Dropshipping Website — Polished Requirement Specification

Overview
The students in our college have a lot of unnecessary goods lying around, whereas many other people might be benefitted from those things. So I would like to put forward a website that can be used to sell or trade the goods with someone who needs them. It proves to be extremely useful in cases like buying and trading second- hand books or study materials among students and faculties.

Operational Context
Primary actors include customer, seller, user, students.
