Equihealth — Polished Requirement Specification

Overview
This case study mainly focusses on developing an application to address the health disparities among the various classes of patients and the appropriate medical interventions in order to achieve health equity for every individual. The target users of the application are patients, caretakers, healthcare providers, medical professionals and government officials. One of the main functionalities of the system is a patient classification based on social determinants such as economic backgrounds, health insurance amount received, medical allowances, demographics, age, type of disability and hereditary health information.

Operational Context
Primary actors include patient, user, authority.

Functional Requirements
1. The system shall classify patients based on their social determinants.
2. The system shall verify patient data entered by the patient with details furnished by hospital medical records.
3. The system shall notify patients about relevant medical events and health schemes based on their categorized data.
4. The system shall provide personal health-tracking facilities for patients by assessing individual medical records.
5. The system shall enable healthcare providers to plan and schedule health worker activities based on patient needs.
6. The system shall provide data analysis for government officials to assist in implementing intervention schemes to offer medical facilities without conflicts and eliminate health discrepancies.
7. The system shall register patients with unique credentials and categorize them as patients, health workers, or government authorities.
8. The system shall provide login functionality for users to access the application using their unique credentials.
9. The system shall include a logout option for users to log out of the application at any time.
10. The system shall provide a data entry section for patients to enter their medical and social details, including unique identification details.
11. The system shall provide a patient dashboard with articles related to the patient's present medical conditions and a search bar to search for specific articles.
12. The system shall provide a health worker dashboard with informative articles pertaining to their area of specialization.
13. The system shall provide a health-tracking facility with detailed analytics of patient individual health records, including data synchronization from other health monitoring applications.
14. The system shall schedule alerts for all users and dispatch them to patients and health workers when an activity is due.
15. The system shall schedule health worker activities according to analyzed patient requirements, including tracking progress of planned activities.
16. The system shall analyze data for patterns of medical disparities based on family and community issues and socio-economic determinants, presenting a holistic report accessible to authorized government authorities.
17. The system shall enable the government to implement health financing schemes with various state-level agencies using analytics data to bring about health equity for affected individuals.
