Online Marketplace — Polished Requirement Specification

Overview
Various new small scale and medium scale sellers face difficulties to reach out to the customers and sell their products along with other competing products in the market. This increases their overall cost, thus reducing their profit or even causing loss. E-Retail is an online marketplace application case study where small and medium scale sellers can list, showcase, advertise and sell their products directly to the customers without any physical store.

Operational Context
Primary actors include customer, seller.

Functional Requirements
1. The system shall allow small and medium scale sellers to list their products.
2. The system shall enable sellers to showcase their products.
3. The system shall allow sellers to advertise their products.
4. The system shall display a list of all products available from various sellers for customers to view.
5. The system shall enable customers to purchase products online.
6. The systel shall allow customers to make online payments.
7. The system shall allow customers to rate or give reviews for products.
8. The system shall enable customers to report sellers for any violations in delivery or non-compliance with product specifications.
9. The system shall display the contact information and rating of sellers to customers before purchase.
10. The system shall facilitate easy authentication and sign-up for new sellers.
11. The system shall provide separate logins for sellers and customers.
