Healthcare Portal — Polished Requirement Specification

Overview
The major functionalities of this system along with their description are as follows: Register – this functionality acts as a membership functionality that allows the users, patients or caregivers to register into this website to access all the resources. Login – this functionality authenticates the user to provide access permissions. Facilities – this functionality allows us to access all the options available on the website.

Operational Context
Primary actors include patient, caregivers, doctor, user.

Functional Requirements
1. The system shall allow registration of users, patients, and caregivers to access all website resources.
2. The system shall authenticate users to provide access permissions.
3. The system shall grant access to all options available on the website.
4. The system shall terminate user access to the website upon logout.
5. The system shall enable users to purchase insurance and pay for healthcare services consumed.
6. The system shall provide detailed information on all diseases.
7. The system shall facilitate online delivery of medicines, considering doctor's statements and patient's willingness.
8. The system shall provide information on health awareness and prevailing threats.
9. The system shall manage payment for services consumed, including doctor's fees and online medicine delivery charges.
10. The system shall collect user reviews for the website through feedback mechanisms.
