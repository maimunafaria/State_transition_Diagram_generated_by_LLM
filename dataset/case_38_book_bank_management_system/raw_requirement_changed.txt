A person can start by registering, logging in, searching for books, getting books, or buying books.

If they register, they first enter their details. If the details are correct, they receive a member ID and complete the registration. If the details are wrong, they have to enter them again. After registration, they can log in.

To log in, they enter their member ID and password. If both are correct, they can continue. If not, they cannot log in.

When searching for a book, they enter the book details. If the book is found, suggestions are shown. If it is not found, they choose another option.

To get a book, they enter the book ID and then their member ID. If their membership is active, they can get the book. If their membership is inactive, they must pay the fine amount first.

To buy a book, they enter the book ID and see the price. If they pay, they get the book. If they do not pay, it ends there.
