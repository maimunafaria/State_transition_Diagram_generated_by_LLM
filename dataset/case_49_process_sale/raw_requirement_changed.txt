The system starts by waiting for a new sale. Once a sale begins, items are entered one by one as the customer selects them.

After all items are added, the system moves to payment. If the customer pays with cash, the sale is completed immediately.

If the customer chooses to pay by card or check, the system first verifies the payment. Once it is approved, the sale is completed.

After finishing the payment, the system returns to waiting for the next customer.
