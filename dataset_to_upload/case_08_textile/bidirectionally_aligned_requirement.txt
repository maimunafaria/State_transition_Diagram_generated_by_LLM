A customer starts by creating an account by filling in the required details. Once the registration is complete, they can place an order for garments.

After placing the order, there are two possibilities. Either a sample is needed, or the available stock is checked.

If a sample is required, sample materials are sent out. After that, the process continues.

If stock is checked, the availability of raw materials is reviewed. Based on that, if the materials are not available, they are purchased. If the materials are already available, they are processed.

Once the raw materials are ready, either through purchasing or processing, garment production begins.

After the garments are produced, they are checked for any defects. Once everything is cleared, the finished garments are delivered to the customer.

After delivery, billing is completed, and the payment is made.

Finally, a report is generated along with feedback, and the process comes to an end.
