A person can start by either exploring blogs and news updates or by creating an account.

If they choose to view blogs and news, they can read the available updates and leave whenever they want.

If they decide to create an account, they provide their details, which are then checked. If the details are not accepted, they cannot continue. If everything is correct, they sign in using their password. If the password is incorrect, they cannot continue. If it is correct, they can view case-related information and logs.

After signing in, the person can choose what they want to do next. They may add a new case, file something online, download model forms, look into references, or give feedback.

If they choose to add a case, they first select a category. The case can belong to either civil or criminal. After choosing the category, they enter the case details. Once the details are added, they can set a reminder and then upload the related documents.

If they choose to file online, they select a category and follow a link that takes them to another location, after which they come back.

If they choose to download model forms, they select the form they need and download it.

If they go to the reference section, they select a category and then choose between quick references or books to view the information.

If they decide to give feedback, they write and submit their feedback.

After finishing what they want to do, they leave the system.
