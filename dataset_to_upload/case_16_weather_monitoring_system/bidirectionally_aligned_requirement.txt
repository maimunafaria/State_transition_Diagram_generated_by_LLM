A person can start either by creating an account or by logging in if already registered.

If the person is new, they go through registration. If the registration is not successful, it ends there. If it is successful, they continue to log in.

After logging in, the person can check the weather either for their current location or for another location. If they enter a new location and it is not valid, it ends there. If the location is valid, they continue.

Once the location is set, the person chooses what they want to check, such as temperature, humidity, rainfall, pressure, or dew point.

After that, the information is updated and shown. From there, the person can choose to see alerts, a weekly analysis, a future prediction, or a graph.

Once the selected information is prepared, it is displayed to the person.

After getting the required details, the person logs out, and it ends.
