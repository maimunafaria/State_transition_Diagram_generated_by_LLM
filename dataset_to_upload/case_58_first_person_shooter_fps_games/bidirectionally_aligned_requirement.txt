A troll in a game normally stays calm and does nothing when there is no threat around. As soon as it notices an opponent, its reaction depends on how strong the opponent appears.

If the opponent looks weak enough to defeat, the troll becomes aggressive and starts attacking. It continues fighting as long as it can see the opponent. If it loses sight of the opponent, it calms down and returns to its normal behavior.

On the other hand, if the opponent seems too strong, the troll becomes scared and panics instead of fighting. If it manages to get away or loses sight of the opponent, it eventually calms down and returns to normal.

During any encounter, if the troll takes a fatal hit and its health drops to zero, it dies and is removed from the game.
