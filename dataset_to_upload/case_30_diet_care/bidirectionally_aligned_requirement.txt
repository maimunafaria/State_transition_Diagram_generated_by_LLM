A user can either enter their information for analysis or choose to consult a professional nutritionist.

If the user chooses consultation, they speak with the nutritionist and can rate the experience afterward.

If the user enters their information, the data is analyzed. Based on the result, the user may be identified as having an unhealthy diet, having a disease or deficiency, or already following a healthy diet.

If the user’s diet is unhealthy, suitable food suggestions are provided. If a disease or deficiency is found, food suggestions are also provided to help with that condition.

If the user is already following a healthy diet, they are simply appreciated for it.
