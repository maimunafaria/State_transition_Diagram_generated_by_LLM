A person starts by providing their citizenship status.

If the person is not an Indian citizen, they cannot continue. If they are an Indian citizen, their details are checked.

Once the details are verified, the person registers using their ID and receives a unique health ID. Using this ID, they sign in to their account.

If the login is successful, the account is activated.

After activation, the person has two options. They can either add their old files or continue by setting permissions.

If they choose to add old files, those files are stored.

If they choose to set permissions, access is provided to the relevant authorities. After that, the person’s files may be accessed if permission is given. If permission is not given, nothing further happens.

When access is allowed, new files can be added, and everything is stored.

At the same time, the stored data can be accessed by the government to view details and prepare reports.

All the information is finally stored, and the process ends.
