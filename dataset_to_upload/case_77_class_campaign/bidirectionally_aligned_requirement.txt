A campaign begins after a manager and staff are assigned to it. Once everything is approved and the contract is signed, the campaign becomes active and starts running.

When the campaign is finished, a final statement is prepared and the process moves toward completion. After that, payment is handled.

If the payment is less than what is due, a refund is generated. If the payment is more, the system waits until the correct amount is settled. When the payment matches exactly, the campaign is marked as fully paid.

After everything is settled, the campaign is archived and the assigned staff and manager are released.
