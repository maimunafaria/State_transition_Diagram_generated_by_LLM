A user begins by logging in. After signing in, they reach the main area where they can choose what they want to check.

From there, they can look at market conditions, labour availability, weather insights, soil-related suggestions, pest control help, or log out.

If they choose market conditions, they are shown the market report and can then continue using the service.

If they choose pest control, they provide the required photo, and once the details are accepted, they are shown pest control techniques.

If they choose soil-related help, they provide soil information, and once the details are accepted, they are shown which crops are suitable.

If they choose weather insights, they provide their location, and once the location is available, they are shown the weather analysis.

If they choose labour availability, they provide their location, and once the location is available, they are shown the labour availability details.

After viewing any of these results, the user can continue using the service again. If they choose to log out, the session ends.
