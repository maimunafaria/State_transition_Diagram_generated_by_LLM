Graderate — Polished Requirement Specification

Functional Requirements
1. The system shall keep the rate in a waiting state until the start date arrives.
2. The system shall activate the rate once the current date equals or surpasses its start date.
3. The system shall maintain the rate's active state until the end of its validity period.
4. The system shall mark the rate as expired once the finish date is reached.
5. The system shall remove or archive the rate after one year from its expiration date.
