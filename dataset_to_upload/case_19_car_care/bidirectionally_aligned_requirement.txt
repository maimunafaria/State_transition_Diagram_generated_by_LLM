A person starts by choosing whether they are using the service center side or the car owner side .

If they are a service center, they can either register or log in if already registered. After logging in, they reach their home area where they can enter damaged parts, update parts information, or send expiry-related notifications. After finishing any of these, they can continue using the system or log out.

If they are a car owner, they can also register or log in. After logging in, they reach their home area where they can view parts information, request or respond to services, or report a service center.

If they choose a service, they can select the required parts and make a payment, and then continue.

After completing their activities, they can log out, and it ends.
