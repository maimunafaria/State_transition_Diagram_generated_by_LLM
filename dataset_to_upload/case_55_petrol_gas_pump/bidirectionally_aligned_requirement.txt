When a petrol pump is not being used, it shows a welcome message and waits for a customer. Once a customer inserts their card, the system reads the card details and checks if everything is valid.

If the card is valid, the system prepares the pump for use and allows the customer to start fueling. If the card is invalid, an error message is shown, and the system resets back to waiting.

When everything is ready, the customer can lift the nozzle and start fueling. Fuel is delivered as long as the nozzle is in use. If the customer stops pressing the nozzle, the fuel delivery pauses.

After fueling is finished and the nozzle is returned, the system processes the payment and deducts the amount from the customer’s account.

Once the payment is completed, the system returns to its initial state, ready for the next customer.

If at any point the process takes too long without activity, the system automatically resets and goes back to waiting.
