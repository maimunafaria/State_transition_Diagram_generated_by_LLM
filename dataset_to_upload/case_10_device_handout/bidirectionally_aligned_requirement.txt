A person starts by registering as a donor by entering their personal details. These details are checked, and once everything is fine, their contact information is collected.

After that, the donor shares details about the device they want to donate. These details are then reviewed.

Based on this review, a decision is made.

If the device meets the requirements, it is accepted. An executive is assigned, and the details are shared with them. The executive then picks up the device and confirms its status.

If the device does not meet the requirements, the donor is informed that it cannot be accepted, and the process ends.
