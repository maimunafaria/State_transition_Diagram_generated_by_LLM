A person starts by choosing their role, such as investor, entrepreneur, or mentor, and then signs in.

After signing in, a startup idea is submitted and reviewed. A decision is then made on that idea.

Based on the decision, one of several things can happen. The person may move forward with mentorship, or the idea may be presented to an investor. If it is presented, an investment decision is made, and funding may be provided.

In some cases, the process may also end directly after the decision.

Once everything is done, the process comes to an end.
