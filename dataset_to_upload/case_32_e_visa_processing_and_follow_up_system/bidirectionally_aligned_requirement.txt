A person can start by creating an account or by logging in.

If they create an account first, they can then log in.

After logging in, they can apply for a visa, view the status, check the status, cancel the application, modify their details, view their details, or view the purpose of the application.

If they apply for a visa, they fill in the application and then make the payment. Once the payment is done, the application is accepted and its status is updated.

If they choose to view the purpose of the application, the documents are checked after that. Based on the document check, the visa is either approved or rejected, and then the status is updated.

If they view the status, the status is updated afterward.

If they check the status, they can then give feedback.

If they cancel the application, modify their details, view their details, give feedback, or complete the status update, they then log out.

After logging out, it ends.
