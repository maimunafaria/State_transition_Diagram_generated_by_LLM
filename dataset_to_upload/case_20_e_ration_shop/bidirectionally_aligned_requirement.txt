A person can either raise a complaint directly or continue with authentication.

If they choose to raise a complaint, they fill in the details and submit it, and it ends there.

If they choose authentication, they verify their identity using an OTP. If the OTP does not match, they try again. Once it is correct, they can continue.

After that, they can view items based on different categories and select what they need.

Once items are selected, they proceed to make a purchase and choose how to pay.

They can pay either through an online method or by cash on delivery. After the payment is completed, the product is delivered, and it ends.
