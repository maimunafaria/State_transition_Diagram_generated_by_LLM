Requirement:
Healthcare Portal — Polished Requirement Specification

Functional Requirements
1. The system shall allow new users to complete registration.
2. The system shall end access if registration fails.
3. The system shall allow registered users to sign in.
4. The system shall provide access to available facilities after signing in.
5. The system shall allow users to read news and expert content.
6. The system shall provide health-related information allowing users to check symptoms or use fitness checking options.
7. The system shall allow users to continue using the system or finish the session after viewing a result.
8. The system shall process and validate insurance for available finance-related services.
9. The system shall check payment with insurance for doctor consultations and complete the payment process.
10. The system shall allow users to select payment methods (cash or insurance) for drug and supplement purchases.
11. The system shall inform users about cash payment details if chosen as the payment method.
12. The system shall complete the payment process for insurance-based payments.
13. The system shall collect user feedback after service completion.
14. The system shall log out users at the end of their session.
