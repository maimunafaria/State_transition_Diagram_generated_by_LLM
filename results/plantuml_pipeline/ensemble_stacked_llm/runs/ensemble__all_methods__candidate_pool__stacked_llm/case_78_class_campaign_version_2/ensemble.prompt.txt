Requirement:
Class Campaign Version 2 — Polished Requirement Specification

Functional Requirements
1. The system shall start a campaign after a manager and staff are assigned.
2. The system shall activate the campaign once everything is approved and the contract is signed.
3. The system shall prepare adverts during the campaign.
4. The system shall approve and schedule the adverts.
5. The system shall run the adverts.
6. The system shall allow extension or adjustment of the campaign while it is running.
7. The system shall complete the campaign and prepare a final statement after all adverts have finished.
8. The system shall handle payment after the campaign completion and generate a refund if the amount paid is less than required.
9. The system shall wait for the correct balance to be settled if the amount paid exceeds the required amount.
10. The system shall mark the campaign as paid once the full amount is properly paid.
11. The system shall archive the campaign and release all assigned staff and managers.
