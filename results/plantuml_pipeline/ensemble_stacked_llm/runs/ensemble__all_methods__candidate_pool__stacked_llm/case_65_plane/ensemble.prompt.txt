Requirement:
Plane — Polished Requirement Specification

Functional Requirements
1. The system shall place a newly acquired plane in an 'Order' state until it is delivered.
2. The system shall transition a plane from 'Arrival' to 'Preparation' upon its arrival.
3. The system shall assign a prepared plane to either 'Operations' or 'Maintenance' based on its condition and schedule.
4. The system shall keep a plane in the 'Operations' state if it still has scheduled flights.
5. The system shall send a plane to 'Maintenance' when there are no more scheduled flights or maintenance is required.
6. The system shall transition a plane from 'Maintenance' to 'Preparation' after checking and servicing it until it is ready for operations.
7. The system shall take a plane out of service if it is no longer available for use.
8. The system shall remove a plane from its operations when it is permanently withdrawn from service.
