Requirement:
Digidoclocker — Polished Requirement Specification

Functional Requirements
1. The system shall identify the type of user based on their entered details.
2. The system shall require citizens to register before logging in.
3. The system shall allow citizens to log in if their login details are correct.
4. The system shall enable citizens to upload documents, view their profile, or apply for a certificate after logging in.
5. The system shall allow citizens to continue uploading documents if needed after initial uploads.
6. The system shall enable citizens to apply for multiple certificates and reapply after sending a request to the issuer.
7. The system shall allow registrars to request certificates for verification.
8. The system shall enable registrars to request multiple certificates and continue requesting more after receiving one.
9. The system shall end the session once the user is done with their actions.
