PARAPHRASE:
The system provides an online healthcare platform for users, patients, and caregivers to access medical services and resources. Users must first register to access the system. After registration, users can log in and use various services available on the website.

The system allows access to healthcare-related functionalities including insurance purchase, service payments, disease information, health calculators, finding doctors, ambulance services, medicine delivery, and health awareness content. Payment is handled through insurance or direct payment methods. After using the services, users can log out of the system.

Functionalities

Register – Allows users to create an account to access system resources.

Login – Authenticates users and grants access permissions.

Facilities – Provides access to system options.

Finance – Enables purchase of insurance and payment for healthcare services.

Health A–Z – Provides disease-related information.

Resources – Includes symptoms checker, health calculator, finding a doctor, insurance guide, and ambulance services.

Drugs and Supplements – Supports online medicine delivery based on doctor’s prescription and patient consent.

Payment – Handles payment for doctor services and medicine delivery.

News and Experts – Provides health awareness updates.

Feedback – Collects user reviews.

Logout – Ends user session.
