RAW:
Various new small scale and medium scale sellers face difficulties to reach out to the customers and
sell their products along with other competing products in the market. This increases their overall
cost, thus reducing their profit or even causing loss. E-Retail is an online marketplace application
case study where small and medium scale sellers can list, showcase, advertise and sell their prod-
ucts directly to the customers without any physical store. E-Retail aims in providing a dynamic
platform for all small and medium scale sellers to reach out and sell their products directly to
all range of customers at a competing price without the need of any physical store. This enables
the sellers to eliminate the cost of rent or maintenance of a physical store and to minimize other
related costs. It also aims in promoting direct communication between the seller and customer,
hence providing a trusted relationship between them. New sellers can easily authenticate and
signup to the portal and sell their products in the portal. The application offers separate login for
sellers and customers, where the sellers and customers can sign in and start using the application.
Customers can view all the products available from various sellers and buy those products. It also
facilitates online payments for purchasing the products. Customers can rate or give a review for the
product and seller. Customers can also report a seller for any violations in delivery or if the product
does not meet the specified requirement. Customers can also view the contact information and the
rating of the seller before buying the product (Figures 9.1–9.9).


PARAPHRASE:
E-Retail is an online marketplace application designed to support small-scale and medium-scale sellers in reaching customers directly. Many sellers face difficulties in marketing and selling their products in a competitive market, which increases operational costs and reduces profit. The E-Retail platform provides a digital marketplace where sellers can list, showcase, advertise, and sell their products without maintaining a physical store.

The system aims to reduce costs such as rent and maintenance while enabling sellers to offer products at competitive prices. It also promotes direct communication between sellers and customers to build a trusted relationship.

System Functionalities
1. Seller Authentication and Registration

New sellers can authenticate and sign up on the portal. Once registered, sellers can list and manage their products on the platform.

2. Separate Login for Sellers and Customers

The application provides separate login access for sellers and customers. Both sellers and customers can sign in and begin using the system according to their roles.

3. Product Listing and Viewing

Sellers can list, showcase, and advertise their products. Customers can view all available products from different sellers.

4. Product Purchase and Online Payment

Customers can buy products through the platform. The system facilitates online payment for purchasing products.

5. Rating and Review

Customers can rate and provide reviews for both the product and the seller after purchase.

6. Reporting Sellers

Customers can report a seller in case of violations in delivery or if the product does not meet the specified requirements.

7. Seller Information Access

Before purchasing a product, customers can view the seller’s contact information and rating.
