Title: Generic Approval Workflow Rules

Purpose:
Model common approval pipelines such as request approval, claim approval, and admin approval.

States:
- Draft
- Submitted
- Under Review
- Approved
- Rejected
- Cancelled

Events:
- submit
- assign_reviewer
- request_changes
- resubmit
- approve
- reject
- cancel

Required transitions:
- Draft --> Submitted on submit.
- Submitted --> Under Review when reviewer is assigned.
- Under Review --> Approved or Rejected as decision outcomes.
- Rejected --> Draft when edits and resubmission are allowed.
- Cancellation should be possible from non-terminal pre-decision states when requirement permits.

Forbidden transitions:
- Do not jump directly Draft --> Approved unless explicitly stated.
- Do not allow Approved --> Draft without a separate revocation process.
- Avoid overlapping decision transitions with identical event labels.

Terminal states:
- Approved and Cancelled are typically terminal.
- Rejected can be terminal or resumable depending on requirement.

PlantUML mini example:
@startuml
[*] --> Draft
Draft --> Submitted : submit
Submitted --> UnderReview : assign_reviewer
UnderReview --> Approved : approve
UnderReview --> Rejected : reject
Rejected --> Draft : resubmit
Submitted --> Cancelled : cancel
UnderReview --> Cancelled : cancel
Approved --> [*]
Cancelled --> [*]
@enduml
