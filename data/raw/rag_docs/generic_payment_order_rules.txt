Title: Generic Payment and Order Rules

Purpose:
Model order and payment lifecycles in ecommerce or service transactions.

States:
- Cart
- Order Placed
- Payment Pending
- Payment Authorized
- Paid
- Payment Failed
- Fulfillment Pending
- Fulfilled
- Cancelled
- Refunded

Events:
- place_order
- initiate_payment
- authorize
- capture
- fail
- retry
- cancel
- dispatch
- deliver
- refund

Required transitions:
- Cart --> OrderPlaced on place_order.
- OrderPlaced --> PaymentPending when payment starts.
- PaymentPending --> Paid through authorize/capture path.
- PaymentPending --> PaymentFailed when charge fails.
- PaymentFailed --> PaymentPending on retry.
- Paid --> FulfillmentPending before dispatch.
- FulfillmentPending --> Fulfilled on delivery completion.
- Cancellation and refund paths depend on policy and stage.

Forbidden transitions:
- Do not move PaymentFailed directly to Fulfilled.
- Do not allow Cancelled --> Paid.
- Do not allow Refunded --> Fulfilled unless explicit re-open flow exists.

Terminal states:
- Fulfilled, Cancelled, and Refunded are common terminal outcomes.

PlantUML mini example:
@startuml
[*] --> Cart
Cart --> OrderPlaced : place_order
OrderPlaced --> PaymentPending : initiate_payment
PaymentPending --> Paid : authorize_and_capture
PaymentPending --> PaymentFailed : fail
PaymentFailed --> PaymentPending : retry
Paid --> FulfillmentPending : dispatch
FulfillmentPending --> Fulfilled : deliver
OrderPlaced --> Cancelled : cancel
Paid --> Refunded : refund
Fulfilled --> [*]
Cancelled --> [*]
Refunded --> [*]
@enduml
