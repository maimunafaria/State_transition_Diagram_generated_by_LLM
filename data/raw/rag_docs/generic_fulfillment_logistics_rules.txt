Title: Generic Fulfillment and Logistics Rules

Purpose:
Model shipping and logistics status flow for generalized delivery pipelines.

States:
- Created
- Packed
- Dispatched
- In Transit
- Out For Delivery
- Delivered
- Delivery Failed
- Returned

Events:
- pack
- dispatch
- handover_to_carrier
- mark_out_for_delivery
- confirm_delivery
- delivery_attempt_failed
- reattempt
- return_to_sender

Required transitions:
- Created --> Packed before dispatch.
- Packed --> Dispatched before transit.
- Dispatched --> InTransit after carrier handoff.
- InTransit --> OutForDelivery before final attempt.
- OutForDelivery --> Delivered on success.
- OutForDelivery --> DeliveryFailed on failed attempt.
- DeliveryFailed can retry or return.

Forbidden transitions:
- Do not jump Created --> Delivered.
- Do not transition Returned --> OutForDelivery without explicit re-shipment.

Terminal states:
- Delivered and Returned are terminal outcomes.

PlantUML mini example:
@startuml
[*] --> Created
Created --> Packed : pack
Packed --> Dispatched : dispatch
Dispatched --> InTransit : handover_to_carrier
InTransit --> OutForDelivery : mark_out_for_delivery
OutForDelivery --> Delivered : confirm_delivery
OutForDelivery --> DeliveryFailed : delivery_attempt_failed
DeliveryFailed --> OutForDelivery : reattempt
DeliveryFailed --> Returned : return_to_sender
Delivered --> [*]
Returned --> [*]
@enduml
