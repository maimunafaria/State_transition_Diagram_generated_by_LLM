State Machine Best Practices

1. Keep state names short but meaningful.
2. Use consistent action/event naming: verb or verb+object.
3. Every state should have at least one incoming or outgoing transition.
4. Avoid redundant states where possible.
5. Ensure transitions follow realistic domain logic.
6. Avoid cycles unless necessary.
7. Ensure every event leads to a valid target state.
8. Use final states only when the process naturally terminates.
9. Avoid clutter; maintain clarity.
